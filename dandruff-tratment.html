Understanding Dandruff and Scalp Conditions

Dandruff is a recurring condition of the scalp that produces white flakes and itchiness from the rapid fall of dead skin cells. Dandruff, if not treated, may become increasingly severe and result in redness and loss of hair.

Other common scalp conditions include :

Common Causes and Triggers

Overgrowth of Malassezia yeast
